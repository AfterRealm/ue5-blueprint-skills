## Add Material Slot
**Category:** Materials
**Search:** "Add Material Slot"
**Target:** Nanite Assembly Builder

**Inputs:**
- In (exec)
- Target (object)
- Material (object)
- Material Slot Group (integer)
- Slot Name (name)

**Outputs:**
- Out (exec)
- Return Value (integer)

**Description:** Creates a material slot in the specified material slot group with a given material. If the slot name provided is "None", the resulting material slot will take the name of the material.
