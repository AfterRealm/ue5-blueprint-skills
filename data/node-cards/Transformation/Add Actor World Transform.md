## Add Actor World Transform
**Category:** Transformation
**Search:** "Add Actor World Transform"
**Target:** Actor

**Inputs:**
- In (exec)
- Target (object)
- Delta Transform (transform)
- Sweep (boolean)
- Teleport (boolean)

**Outputs:**
- Out (exec)
- Sweep Hit Result (struct)

**Description:** Adds a delta to the transform of this actor in world space. Ignores scale and sets it to (1,1,1).
