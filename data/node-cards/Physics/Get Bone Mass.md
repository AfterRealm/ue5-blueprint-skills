## Get Bone Mass
**Category:** Physics
**Search:** "Get Bone Mass"
**Target:** Skeletal Mesh Component

**Inputs:**
- Target (object)
- Bone Name (name) — Name of the body to return. 'None' indicates root body.
- Scale Mass (boolean) — If true, the mass is scaled by the bone's MassScale.

**Outputs:**
- Return Value (real)

**Description:** Returns the mass (in kg) of the given bone
