## Term Bodies Below
**Category:** Physics
**Search:** "Term Bodies Below"
**Target:** Skeletal Mesh Component

**Inputs:**
- In (exec)
- Target (object)
- Parent Bone Name (name)

**Outputs:**
- Out (exec)

**Description:** Terminate physics on all bodies below the named bone, effectively disabling collision forever. If you terminate, you won't be able to re-init later.
