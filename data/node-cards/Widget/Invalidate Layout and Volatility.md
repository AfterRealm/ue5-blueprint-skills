## Invalidate Layout and Volatility
**Category:** Widget
**Search:** "Invalidate Layout and Volatility"
**Target:** Widget

**Inputs:**
- In (exec)
- Target (object)

**Outputs:**
- Out (exec)

**Description:** Invalidates the widget from the view of a layout caching widget that may own this widget. will force the owning widget to redraw and cache children on the next paint pass.
