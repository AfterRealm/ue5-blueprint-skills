## Reset to Cube
**Category:** DynamicMesh
**Search:** "Reset to Cube"
**Target:** Dynamic Mesh

**Inputs:**
- In (exec)
- Target (object)

**Outputs:**
- Out (exec)
- Target (object)

**Description:** Clear the internal mesh to a 100x100x100 cube with base at the origin. This this instead of Reset() if an initially-empty mesh is undesirable (eg for a Component)
