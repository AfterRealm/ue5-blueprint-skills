## Get Player View Point
**Category:** Pawn
**Search:** "Get Player View Point"
**Target:** Controller

**Inputs:**
- Target (object)

**Outputs:**
- Location (vector)
- Rotation (rotator)

**Description:** Returns Player's Point of View For the AI this means the Pawn's 'Eyes' ViewPoint For a Human player, this means the Camera's ViewPoint @output out_Location, view location of player @output out_rotation, view rotation of player
