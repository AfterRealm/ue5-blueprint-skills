## Move Right
**Category:** Pawn
**Search:** "Move Right"
**Target:** Default Pawn

**Inputs:**
- In (exec)
- Target (object)
- Val (real) — Amount of movement in the right direction (or left if negative).

**Outputs:**
- Out (exec)

**Description:** Input callback to strafe right in local space (or left if Val is negative).
