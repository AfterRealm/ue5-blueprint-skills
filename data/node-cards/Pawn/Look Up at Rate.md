## Look Up at Rate
**Category:** Pawn
**Search:** "Look Up at Rate"
**Target:** Default Pawn

**Inputs:**
- In (exec)
- Target (object)
- Rate (real) — This is a normalized rate, i.e. 1.0 means 100% of desired turn rate

**Outputs:**
- Out (exec)

**Description:** Called via input to look up at a given rate (or down if Rate is negative).
